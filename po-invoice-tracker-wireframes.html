How this is calculated. For every client PO, the system rolls up linked vendor POs (the ones you raise to fulfil that client PO). Revenue = client PO taxable value. Vendor cost = sum of linked vendor POs taxable values (or actual vendor invoice values once received). Margin = Revenue − Vendor cost − Other cost. Cash position is computed from receipts (client side) minus payments (vendor side, net of TDS).

Pay before invoice is received. TDS is deducted at this point of payment (per S.51 / 194-series).
